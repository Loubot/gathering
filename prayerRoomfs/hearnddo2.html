I pray to both hear and do your word…

Lord, it’s a part of your love for us that you want me to share in the doing of your word. I know that you could speak and all that you spoke would be done, yet you want me to share in your work of doing. Thank you for this opportunity to be a part of what you are doing in this world.

And now - I pray that I would both hear and bring forth, as in bring to birth, your spoken word to me into my world.

I pause to be with this truth. And I pray - ‘God please speak to me in the silence.’
